Welcome to UrbanChain

We will make electricity so cheap that only the rich will burn candles.

Urbanchain is the 1st blockchain-based energy supplier (B-Supplier) in the UK and worldwide. We have developed an innovative energy exchange system, harnessing the power of blockchain, AI and IoT, in order to create local energy markets. Our solution offers significant savings in energy bills for consumers, brings renewable energy generators of all size to the mainstream market, and enables large consumers to meet their net zero carbon emission target. We have regulated our innovation with Ofgem, Innovation Link, and are rolling out to the UK energy market in the first instance.

Our Innovation

We aim to disrupt the energy market to empower communities
eChain
Blockchain/AI 4 Local Energy Market

eChain is Blockchain/AI-based platform through which generators and end-users would be able to directly get connected. This makes a two sided platform to connect both sides together and reduce unnecessary shared and search costs.

AI 4 FP
AI 4 Fuel Poverty

AI for fuel poverty (AI4FP) is a cloud based analytical software harnessing AI technology to detect potential and actual fuel poor households in the UK. It is a unique algorithm to automatically detect fuel poor households.
